System Pipeline

From impact to claim — automated

Hardware detects the incident. AI processes the scene. Your claim is filed before you've finished calling home.

01 / DETECT

Collision & Parking Detection

ESP32-S3 + IMU sensor detects impacts and unexpected movement 24/7, even while parked. Hit-and-runs captured automatically.

02 / ANALYZE

AI Scene Understanding

YOLO object detection identifies vehicles, damage severity, and incident type. Gemini Vision generates a structured incident report automatically.

03 / VOICE

Multilingual Voice Triage

AI agent speaks your language — Chinese, Spanish, Korean, Hindi. Guides you step-by-step through what to do right now.

04 / DOCUMENT

Auto-Generated Report

Timestamped footage, GPS location, damage analysis, and incident summary compiled into a single shareable document.

05 / CLAIM

Insurance Claim Filing

Connects directly to your insurance provider via Twilio. Submits documentation and tracks claim status — no English required.

06 / APPEAL

Platform Appeal Support

Uber, Lyft, and platform deactivation appeals auto-drafted using your incident evidence. Fight back with facts.
